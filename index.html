About Us

Yantrik club is a group of undergraduate students from different departments who design and fabricate an electric formula race car, aircraft models. We innovate, design, and excel! We provide a platform for expressing and implementing students’ ideas in the field of Mechanical Engineering. Provide them with enough knowledge to tackle with mechanical and design aspects of everyday problems.
